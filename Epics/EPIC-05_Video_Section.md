# EPIC-05: Video Section
**Priority:** P2 – High | **Sprint:** Week 3 | **Depends On:** EPIC-02

## Description
Video gallery organized by subject/topic. Each video card shows a YouTube thumbnail, title, and description. Clicking redirects to YouTube. Admin manages video entries via admin portal (EPIC-02).

## Acceptance Criteria
- Videos displayed as cards with thumbnail, title, and description
- Videos organized and filterable by subject and standard
- Clicking a card opens the YouTube video in a new tab
- Admin can add, edit, and remove video entries

## User Stories

| ID | Story | Points |
|----|-------|--------|
| US-18 | **Browse Videos by Subject** – As a student, I want to browse videos by subject to find relevant content | 5 |
| US-19 | **Open YouTube Video** – As a student, I want to click a video card and watch it on YouTube | 3 |
| US-20 | **Admin Manages Videos** – As an admin, I want to add, edit, and remove video entries | 5 |
