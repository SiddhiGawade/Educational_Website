# EPIC-06: Blog Section
**Priority:** P2 – High | **Sprint:** Week 3 | **Depends On:** EPIC-02

## Description
Educational blog with posts organized by categories and tags, with search functionality. Admin manages posts via admin portal (EPIC-02).

## Acceptance Criteria
- Blog posts displayed with title, excerpt, date, and category
- Students can search posts by keyword
- Students can read full blog posts with rich formatting
- Posts filterable by category and tags
- Admin can create, edit, and delete posts via rich text editor

## User Stories

| ID | Story | Points |
|----|-------|--------|
| US-21 | **Browse Blog Posts** – As a student, I want to browse educational blog posts | 5 |
| US-22 | **Search Blog Posts** – As a student, I want to search posts by keyword | 3 |
| US-23 | **Read Full Post** – As a student, I want to read the full article on a dedicated page | 3 |
| US-24 | **Admin Manages Blog** – As an admin, I want to create, edit, and delete blog posts | 8 |
| US-25 | **Filter by Category/Tags** – As a student, I want to filter posts by category or tag | 3 |
