## Description

Build a blog section featuring educational articles organized by categories and tags. Students can browse, search, and read blog posts that supplement their classroom learning. The blog serves as a knowledge repository that grows over time.

---

## Business Value

- Provides supplementary learning content beyond quizzes and videos
- SEO-friendly content drives organic traffic
- Categories and search improve discoverability
- Establishes the platform as an educational resource hub

---

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Blog posts are displayed in a list/grid with title, excerpt, date, and category
2. Posts can be filtered by category and tags
3. A search bar allows students to find posts by keyword
4. Individual blog post pages display full content with proper formatting
5. Admin can create, edit, and delete blog posts
